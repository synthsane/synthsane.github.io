Kawai SX-240

Preface

I usually don’t write prefaces, but this case is a little bit special. First thing’s first, a little warning seems to be in place: this may be the one - most adored and beloved synthesizer in my collection. For most people out there, the synonym to vintage analog synthesizer is Roland Juno 6/60/106 and that’s cool. But all in all, this is my site with my opinions and I dare to say:

Kawai SX-240 is epitome of vintage analog synth for me.

Thus, this “review” may be a biased one. (But hey, isn’t the subjectivity what matters?)
